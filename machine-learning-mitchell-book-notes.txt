Definition: Learning (broadly defined)

    A computer program is said to learn from experience E
    with respect to some class of tasks T
    as measured by some performance criteria P
    if its performance in tasks T,
    as measured by P,
    improves with experience E.

credit assignment:  determining the extent to which individual choices in a sequence
  result in the final outcome.
